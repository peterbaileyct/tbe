Outages at 1,300 by Saturday evening

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2690 Published on 2020-08-08 19:13:32

Eversources' online outage map of Bristol and the complementary list of outages on a separate page show 1,337 waiting to have power resourced at 7 p.m. Saturday evening. Pockets remain, such as the one on Vantana Dr. with 43 homes. Projected time for power to be restored to Vantana Dr. is Tuesday.
