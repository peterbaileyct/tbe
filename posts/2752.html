Update: Eversource figures as of 8 p.m.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2752 Published on 2020-08-11 20:30:49

Eversource reports outages of 42 residences or .14 percent of the population as of 8 p.m. this evening, but this figure does not take into account 141 residents in the area of Meiklem St. where Eversource has had a crew for most of the day.

The outage information states that the damage is being assessed and a restoration time will be provided as soon as possible.
