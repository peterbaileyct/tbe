Donna Lazar

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8433 Published on 2021-10-06 18:29:30

Donna (Enaghioaia) Lazar, 74, of Bristol, passed away Sept. 30, at Ingraham Manor, Bristol. She was the wife of the late Dan Lazar.

She was born in Bucharest, Romania, on Nov. 11, 1946, the daughter of the late Ion and Ioana Enaghioaia.

She immigrated to the United States from Romania in June 1982 and became a proud U.S. citizen.

She worked in various industries including financial, hospitality, and caregiver.

She is survived by her son, Julien Lazar and his wife Jessica, and grandchild Kayla from Bristol; sister Nina, and niece Michelle.

A Memorial service will be held on Friday, Oct. 8, at 11 a.m. at Dunn Funeral Home, 191 West St. Bristol, CT 06010.
