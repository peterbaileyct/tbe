Hispanic Heritage Month will be celebrated with a live concert at Rockwell Park

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7692 Published on 2021-09-03 11:01:04

For the first time, Bristol will celebrate Hispanic Heritage month with a live concert at Rockwell Park on September 19th.

Any people who have Latinx/Hispanic heritage and would like to have a table showcasing a country/culture, should contact Lauren Bent, Arts & Culture Supervisor, Department of Parks, Recreation, Youth and Community Services. P: 860-584-6160 E: LaurenBent@bristolct.gov


This isn’t a time to sell products but to offer education about the many countries and cultures that are to be celebrated.
