Bristol Rotary Club offers grants to Non-Profits

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=4722 Published on 2021-03-08 10:51:31

In keeping with its longstanding tradition of philanthropy, the Bristol Rotary Club is accepting applications for grants to non-profit organizations for programs that benefit children in the Bristol community.
 
The application, which consists of a short series of questions, can be found in the Club Information/Grants section of the club’s website (www.bristolrotaryclub.org ).

Completed applications can be emailed to bristolctrotary@gmail.com or mailed to Bristol Rotary Club, P.O. Box 1674, Bristol, CT 06011-1674. Applications are due to the Club by April 15.

The Bristol Rotary Club has awarded mini-grants since 1987. Past recipients include the Bristol Girls Little League Softball program, the American Clock & Watch Museum, the Barnes Nature Center, Shepard Meadows Therapeutic Riding Center, and the Bristol Library.


For more information about the Bristol Rotary Club, visit our website at www.bristolrotaryclub.org or find us on Facebook @BristolCTRotary.
