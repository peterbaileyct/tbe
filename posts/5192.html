Eastern girls tennis falls to New Britain in CCC Interdivisional play

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=5192 Published on 2021-04-21 11:00:54

By Michael Letendre

NEW BRITAIN – The Bristol Eastern girls tennis team fell to 0-2 on the campaign, falling at New Britain by a 6-1 final on Tuesday.

The match, contested from the tennis courts at New Britain high school, was a CCC Interdivisional tilt that saw the Lancers’ top doubles squad pick up their lone victory.

The Eastern combination of Jenna Cefaly and Brianna Kelley defeated the team of Mellea Echevarria and Layla Sahoobah by scores of 6-3 and 6-4 for the win.

Sticking with doubles play, Eastern’s duo Adrianna Reay and Trish Mohan fell to Vicky Rasheshan and Natalia Marcarski by scores of 6-3 and 6-3 and then in the No. 3 slot, New Britain’s Isabella Estanero and Nina Sighavong beat the Lancers’ team of Kiana Ouellette and Samantha Rivard (6-0, 6-3).

And the Hurricanes swept singles action, taking all four matches.

In the top spot, Julitta Oliver defeated Eastern’s Ashlee Lewandoski by scores of 6-0 and 6-1 while in No. 2 singles play, New Britain’s Audrey Belliveau turned away Brooke Treannie (6-2, 6-3) in a competitive bout.

Also for the Hurricanes, Amelina Dananisa beat the Lancers’ Olivia Dossias by scores of 6-0 and 6-2.

And in No. 4 singles’ action, Isabella Petrisko defeated Ava Gesner 6-2 and 6-3 to help the home team take the match.

The Lancers are next scheduled to square off against Platt on Friday in a CCC South encounter from the tennis courts at Bristol Eastern.

Match time is 3:45 p.m.
