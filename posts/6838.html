Farmington Ave. convenience stop introduces scoop ice cream

Neighbors Convenience and Grocery adjacent to Harvest Bakery on Farmington Ave. introduced scoop ice cream a few weeks ago.
The convenience store now offers Gifford's ice cream in both cups and cones. Single servings start at $2, cones $3. Sprinkles and waffle cones are extra.
Varieties available range from chocolate, vanilla and strawberry to Caramel Truffle and PB Caramel Cookie.
Neighbors joins a bunch of Bristol locations that offer ice cream by the scoop. Among these are the Ice Cream Churn, North Main St.; Rich's, Hill St.; Dunphy's, Stafford Ave.; Caravel, Farmington Ave.; and the venerable Friendly, Farmington Avenue.
