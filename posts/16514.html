Trash-To-Treasure event at Farmers Market on Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=16514 Published on 2022-08-29 14:11:24

There will be a great chance to add to your furniture collection Saturday morning. The Bristol Public Works Department will have a selection of pieces taken to the Transfer Station available to whoever wants to make a claim.

As they always say, ”One person’s trash is another’s treasure.”
