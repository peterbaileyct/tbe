Latest Covid-19 update 12 June

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2355 Published on 2020-06-13 16:18:56

On Friday, the state of Connecticut coronavirus website reported 583 confirmed cases up from 576 the previous week and 72 total confirmed deaths up one from the previous week in Bristol.

At the same time, the number of confirmed cases in Connecticut totaled 44,994 confirmed cases up from 43,969 the previous week and 4,186 deaths up from 4,071 the previous week.

Also, the state has reported 342,338 COVID-19 PCR tests, around 1.14 percent of the total population.
