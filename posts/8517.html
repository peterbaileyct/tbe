BC Girls swimming finishes second at tri-meet with Bristol Eastern and Farmington

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8517 Published on 2021-10-10 20:40:16

On Thursday night, Bristol Central came in second in a tri-meet against Farmington and Bristol Eastern High School.  

Personal best times for the Rams were achieved by Madison Pikiell in the 200 and 500 free, Milan Barresi in the 200 free, Madison Field in the 200 IM, Isabella Cordero in the 50 free, Meadow Wells in diving, Katelyn Holleran and Christina Tomcak in the 100 free, and Hannah Krueger and Taylor Sisson in the 500 free.

Central Coach Adrienne Bentley said, “I feel the team did well, going up against a powerhouse like Farmington.  It was their second meet in 3 days, they were tired, but wanted to swim fast. And we came away with 16 season best times, of those 14 were personal bests!”

Central swims against Middletown on Friday.
