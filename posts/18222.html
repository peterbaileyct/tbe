Two Bristol police officers killed, one injured, in shooting incident on Redstone Hill Rd.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18222 Published on 2022-10-13 06:16:13

Two Bristol police officers were killed and one was seriously injured in a shooting in the area of Redstone Hill Road and Birch Street, according to state police.

State police confirmed that they responded to the area just after 11 p.m. on reports of an officer-involved shooting.

The state police reported on Twitter that “3 officers were shot in Bristol OIS. We are still working diligently to gather accurate information from our investigators and all that are involved. We will provide a press conference as soon as we can. Again, please be patient as the scene is still very active and fluid”

TBE will update the situation as soon as more information becomes available.
