WINTER PARKING BAN 2020-2021

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=3602 Published on 2020-12-15 20:09:00

In addition to parking bans during winter storms, The City of Bristol’s winter Parking Ban begins December 15, 2020, and will be in effect through March 15, 2021. The parking ban restricts parking on any City of Bristol street between the hours of 2 a.m. and 6 a.m. regardless of weather conditions.
