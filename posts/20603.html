Community Tree Lighting ceremony takes place at Page Park on Thursday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=20603 Published on 2022-12-06 08:14:37

Get your family over to the Page Park pool on Thursday.

The Community Tree Lighting Ceremony will take place beginning at 6:30 p.m., continuing till 8:00.

The Bristol Park, Rec. And Community Services department invites everyone to kick off the holiday season amongst family and friends. You can stroll through Page Park to view the Holiday Lights Display and then join the festivities of caroling, hot chocolate, and visits with Santa!
Then help us count down as we light the tree on the upper deck of Page Park Pool!

Thursday, December 8, 6:30 - 8:00 PM at Page Park
