Eastern's girls tennis squad falls to Maloney, 7-0, in a CCC South tilt on Monday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=5456 Published on 2021-05-11 12:39:32

By Michael Letendre

BRISTOL – The Bristol Eastern girls tennis squad had its hands full against Maloney in a CCC South engagement on Monday in Bristol, dropping a tough 7-0 decision.

With the loss, the Lancers fell to 2-8 on the year.

The best match of the encounter came at No. 2 singles play as Maloney’s Lily Marcuss and Eastern’s Brooke Treannie pushed each other to the limit.

Treannie won the first set 6-4, dropped the second in a tough 7-5 showing – forcing a winner-take-all tiebreaker to determine the ultimate victor.

It was close but Treannie fell, 10-7, in the third set to drop an exciting match.

Samantha Pirruccio defeated Ashlee Lewandoski in No. 1 singles (6-2, 6-0) while Jillian Shapiro beat Olivia Dossias (6-0, 6-0) at No. 3.

And then at No. 4 singles play, Brianna Skeen earned a 6-1, 6-0 victory over Ava Gesner.

In top double’s action, the Spartans duo of Abby and Haley Lespier scooped up a 6-2, 6-1 win against Jenna Cefaly and Brianna Kelley and then in No. 2 doubles, Savannah Bernabucci and Michaela Wrinn defeated Eastern’s Adrianna Reay and Trisha Mohan (6-0, 6-1).

Finally at No. 3, Maloney’s combination of Nancy Nguyenand Sophie Boulier swept the twosome of Gina D’Angelo and Kiana Ouellette 6-0, 6-0.
