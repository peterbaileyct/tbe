Bristol police ask for public's help in investigating accident

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=5182 Published on 2021-04-20 10:24:17

The following is a request issued Monday, 4/19

BRISTOL POLICE DEPARTMENT

131 North Main Street, Bristol CT 06010

Public Information Officer: Lieutenant Geoffrey Lund

Email: GeoffreyLund@BristolCt.gov

Phone: (860) 584-3060

Date of Incident: 04-15-21

Time of Incident: 1:43pm

Incident Case #: 21-12001

Summary:

On April 15, 2021 at approximately 1:43pm, the Bristol Police Department received a report of a motor vehicle crash at the intersection of Burlington Avenue and Maple Avenue. The vehicles involved were a white Chevrolet Silverado and a black Honda Accord.

Officers responded to the scene and the crash is still under investigation.

The Bristol Police are asking for anyone that may have witnessed or have information regarding this incident to contact the Traffic Division at 860-584-3038.
