Bristol police issue missing juvenile alert

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=54040 Published on 2024-11-18 09:03:53

The Bristol Police Department issued a missing person alert on Yandel Quinones-Aponte.

Quinones-Aponte is 13 years old and is 5’1” in height, weighs about 275 lbs. and has brown hair and brown eyes.

If anyone has any information on him, please contact the Bristol Police Department at 860-584-3011.
