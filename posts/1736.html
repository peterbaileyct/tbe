Hospital announces new test collection hours for public

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=1736 Published on 2020-05-03 16:24:50

Beginning Monday, May 4, the hours for the coronavirus test kit collection station at the Bristol Hospital will shift from 8 a.m to 11 a.m. The station will close at 2 p.m., or once 40 kits have been collected, which ever happens first.

The hospital announced the change on its website.

The website also announced that from 8 a.m. to 10 a.m. the hospital will be testing exclusively for pre-op patients.

As of May 2, the state has confirmed that Bristol has had 395 cases of Covid-19 with 51 deaths.
