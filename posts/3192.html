Central's Nate DeAngelo has career day in 5k from New Jersey

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=3192 Published on 2020-11-02 09:10:24

By Michael Letendre

EDISON PARK, NEW JERSEY - Bristol Central boys cross country standout Nate DeAngelo is having a senior season to remember. 

He continues to win races in Central Connecticut Conference, Region B action, but recently DeAngelo traveled outside the state of Connecticut where the speedster continued to excel, continued to impress.

On a warm Halloween afternoon, DeAngelo took a shot at the 5k event from the Garden State Track Club High School Fall Showcase Open event.

And the senior placed near the top of his event.

Several athletes from the Nutmeg State competed in the 5k challenge and DeAngelo earned a medal with an amazing third place finish.

DeAngelo ended the race with a blazing time of 15:01.66 which included a final 66-second lap.

Winning the 5k race was Gavin Sherry of Conard who blitzed the course for a time of 14:30.37 while Jackson Barna (14:35.76) of Basking Ridge, New Jersey was the runner-up.
