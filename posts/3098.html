Eastern volleyball sweeps Plainville in easy fashion

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=3098 Published on 2020-10-22 23:37:30

The Lancer volleyball team ran their record to 8-0 with a quick 3-set sweep of Plainville Thursday in the Eastern gym.

The first set was actually the most competitive of the afternoon as Eastern prevailed, 25-10.

After that, the Lancers were the proverbial runaway freight train, winning sets 2 and 3 by identical scores of 25-4. Coach Stefanie Reay took the opportunity to play everyone on the varsity bench with such a one-sided score. And everyone on the team took full advantage of their chances, with multiple underclass players showing off their skills.

Statistically, the Lancers were led by Amber Champagne with 8 aces, 1 kill and 2 digs. Abby Jones had 4 assists and 2 aces, and Hannah Podolak chipped in with 4 assists.

Records:
Plainville: 0-8

Bristol Eastern: 8-0

For their next match, Eastern travels to Southington on Monday, October 26.
