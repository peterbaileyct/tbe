Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=271 Published on 2024-11-20 09:21:11 The Bristol Edition! What? The Bristol Edition is an online news publication that I have created as part of my masters project with Kent State. The goal is to engage the community in a conversation about what is the news, how the news develops, who determines the news, and what gets printed. The conversation will include ethics and some recent science. More on that later. In other words, BTE is a collaborative effort with the goal of bringing some sanity to our local news by engaging the community and its better angels to meet and talk about the what the news means for our community, and then to provide that news by identifying people willing to learn about journalism and to practice journalism. Engage the community how? TBE engages the community by asking people like you and me to learn to join together, as citizens, to reclaim our journalistic legacy from social media.
