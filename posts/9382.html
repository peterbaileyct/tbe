Bristol Transfer Station extended hours for leaf disposal

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9382 Published on 2021-11-17 08:42:06

The City of Bristol has announced the extended hours available for leaf drop off at the Transfer Station:

November 18th and 19th (Thurs and Fri) 7:15-5:00

November 20th (Sat) 7:30-3:00 

November 26th (Fri) 7:15-5:00

November 27th (Sat) 7:30-3:00

Leaf disposal is FREE with an active Transfer Station permit and required minimum funds on the account.
