Mum Festival welcomes big crowds in perfect weather

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8180 Published on 2021-09-25 19:50:05

The 2021 Mum Festival welcomed throngs of Bristolites in its first two days, aided by perfect weather and an attractive array of entertainment, vendors, amusements, and food.

The Mum Festival ends on Sunday with the ever-popular Mum Parade at 1:30. The parade route is shortened this year, so you will need to arrive early to get a good view.
