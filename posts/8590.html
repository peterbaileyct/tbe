Saturday’s schedule for the 100th Anniversary of Veterans Memorial Boulevard

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8590 Published on 2021-10-15 12:33:38

100th Anniversary of Veterans Memorial Boulevard 
This Saturday, October 16, 2021

1:00 to 5:00 p.m. Veterans Memorial Boulevard

The celebration of the 100th Anniversary of Bristol’s Veterans Memorial Boulevard is this Saturday.

The day’s events will include an opening and closing ceremony, music, a time capsule, historical stations, era cars, presentations at the various monuments, as well as period games and food truck options.

Schedule for the Day: 

1:00 pm - Opening Ceremony

1:30 pm - Performance by Bristol Public School Students

2:00 pm - Walking Stations / Musical Performances by Bristol Brass and Wind Ensemble

4:00 pm - Closing Ceremony

5:00 pm - Centennial Celebration Concludes
