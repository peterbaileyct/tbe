Public Information Meeting scheduled regarding rebuild of Wolcott Street

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7902 Published on 2021-09-13 14:10:33

There will be a Public Information Meeting to review and discuss the proposed project for the reconstruction of Wolcott Street on Wednesday, September 15, 2021 at 7:00 p.m.

The reconstruction project will extend from Route 69 to Witches Rock Road in Bristol.

The meeting will be at the Council Chambers in Bristol City Hall, on 111 North Main Street.
