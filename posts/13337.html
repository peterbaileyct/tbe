Peacedale St. to be closed on Thursday from Hart St. to Burlington Ave.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=13337 Published on 2022-05-17 17:07:18

The Bristol Public Works Department announced they will be closing Peacedale Street from Burlington Avenue to Hart Street on Thursday, May 19, 2022 for drainage repairs.

The road will be open to local traffic.

Detours will be posted and the road will be reopened as soon as possible.

The Public Works Department anticipates having the work completed in one day. Any additional work would not require a road closure.

Any questions should be directed to the Bristol Public Works Department, 860 584-6125.
