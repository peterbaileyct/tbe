Vehicle crash on Middle Street causes road closure

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=34683 Published on 2023-09-28 14:03:12

The Bristol Police Department stated in a press release this afternoon that Middle Street between Ronzo Road and Enterprise Drive is closed to thru traffic due to a downed utility pole caused by a one-vehicle crash. There were no serious injuries reported. Motorists are asked to seek alternate routes for the time being.
