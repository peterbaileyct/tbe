Eastern softball drops 16-1 decision to Berlin Wednesday afternoon in Bristol

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=5483 Published on 2021-05-13 09:47:33

By Michael Letendre

BRISTOL - The softball squad from Bristol Eastern is not a pushover in the least this year.

But occasionally, a bad loss or two can occur along a busy schedule and that’s exactly what happened to the program on Wednesday.

Berlin came to town to square off against the Lancers in a Central Connecticut Conference, South division confrontation.

The Redcoats, one of the top teams in all the CCC, ended up blitzing the Kingstreeters by a 16-1 final from the Eastern softball field.

The game went only five innings as the mercy rule eventually ended play.

It was the ninth straight win for the Redcoats, now 13-1, while Eastern fell to 8-4 off the loss.

However, the Lancers did have a bright spot in the form of Jasmine Perez.

Perez was able to take a ball yard, splashing out a solo home run in the third inning of play to get her team on the scoreboard.

But the Lancers never scored again as the program picked up a tough loss from home.

Eastern won’t have much opportunity to dwell on the setback as the Lancers travel to Platt on Thursday, hoping to start a new winning streak in another CCC South tilt.

Game time is at 3:45 p.m. from the Platt high school diamond in Meriden.
