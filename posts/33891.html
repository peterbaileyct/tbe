Bristol Eastern volleyball opens season with a home win against Plainville

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=33891 Published on 2023-09-12 09:29:34

In a warm Lancerdome at Bristol Eastern, but the Eastern girls proved to be even warmer as they swept Plainville Monday night.

The Lancers made easy work of the first set, jumping out to an early lead and finishing it off quickly by a score of 25-13.

Plainville finally put it together for a while, and made set two a real battle, but Eastern ultimately pulled it out, 26-24.

The Lancers completed the sweep with an easy 25-10 third set clincher.
