Outage on Federal Hill

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=4080 Published on 2021-01-14 20:23:49

One-hundred ninety-one residences on Federal Hill lost power around 7:45 p.m. this evening, Eversource has reported on his power outage map. The site stated that power should be restored around 10:15 p.m. There is no reported source of the outage.
