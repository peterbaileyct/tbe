TBE Sports Sunday: The Bristol Central 2021 Scholastic Sports Award winners

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=6222 Published on 2021-06-27 14:23:14

By Michael Letendre 

The Bristol Central 2021 Virtual Spring Sports Awards Banquet was a tremendous way for the school to cap off the scholastic campaign. 

And numerous players earned postseason awards due to their play on the field and commitment in the classroom. 

Two students from Central earned the 2021 Scholar Athlete Awards as Chris Osuch and Emma Quintero were both honored. 

Also handed out were ‘The Varsity 8 Awards’ that went to Bella Crandall and Natalia Popielarz. 

Now, to the individual sports:
