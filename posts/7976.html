BOE Faces Unmasking Protestors

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7976 Published on 2021-09-15 23:22:40

Before the Bristol Board of Education monthly meeting and upset with the mask mandate in Bristol Public Schools, about 20 protestors, including men, women, and children, stood on the sidewalk outside the Board of Education Building on Church Street, Wednesday just before the sunset.

As a revolutionary war drummer tapped put duty calls on his snare drum, and with American flags waving and noisemakers filling the air, another man armed with a bullhorn called for the resignation of BOE president Jen Dube with catcalls of “Jennifer Dube resign you are a disgrace to Bristol!”

Meanwhile, inside, board members diligently prepared for the monthly meeting unaffected by the showings outside.

The meeting was called to order at approximately 7:02, and it went about its business.  As the board members soldiered through some weighty material such as (), the 800-pound gorilla in the room was the protestors.  Would they arrive, and would there be a disturbance?

Eventually, that question was answered. Several protestors did arrive and took up the limited but remaining seats wearing masks.
