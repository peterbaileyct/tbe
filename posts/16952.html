Bristol NAACP Annual Freedom Fund Banquet to be held on October 28

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=16952 Published on 2022-09-10 14:17:29

The Bristol Branch of the NAACP has announced the date of their annual Freedom Fund Banquet as Friday, October 28. The Banquet will be held at Testa's Banquet Facility in Southington beginning with a social hour at 6:00 and the dinner at 7:00.

This year's keynote speaker will be Mrs. Marlo Greponne, the Executive Director of the Human Resource Agency. The group will also honor Olga Callender at this year's banquet.

Tickets will be available for $80.00. Please contact Lexie Mangum at 860-202-9965 for more information.
