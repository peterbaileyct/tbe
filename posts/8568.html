Tonight the Coloring Craze Party returns to the Bristol Historical Society

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8568 Published on 2021-10-15 11:14:52

Following the return of the Coloring Craze Party in September to the Bristol Historical Society (BHS), coloring enthusiasts can rejoice. Tonight, the Coloring Craze Party is back at BHS from 6:30-8:30 p.m.

Bring your coloring books, supplies, and snacks, while BHS supplies the wine.

If you feel stressed after a long week, this is an opportunity to pick up your colored pencils and be creative. The entrance fee is $5, and while pre-registration is encouraged, walk-ins are welcome. 

In other BHS news, the Children's Scavenger Hunt final date is extended until this Saturday, Oct. 16.

The Bristol Historical Society is located at 98 Summer St., and its website can be found at www.bristolhistoricalsociety.org.
