Christmas Tree collection dates announced

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10081 Published on 2021-12-28 12:32:02

The City of Bristol has announced the dates for Christmas Tree collections this year.

The 2022 Christmas Tree Collection will begin Monday, January 3, 2022 and will end Friday, January 21, 2022. 

During this time, please place trees curbside by 6:00 a.m. on your rubbish collection day. Trees must have all tree stands, lights, ornaments, and tree bags removed. Remember to keep your Christmas tree (or any other object or container) at least five feet from your city rubbish barrel, as well as five feet away from other barrels and obstructions (parked vehicles, telephone phones, fire hydrants, etc.).
