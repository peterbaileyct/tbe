Bristol Eastern volleyball makes quick work of Plainville

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2945 Published on 2020-10-06 23:14:21

Bristol Eastern scored a three-set victory over Plainville Monday afternoon at Plainville High School. The set scores were:
Set 1: BE 25- PHS14
Set 2: BE 25- PHS 12
Set 3: BE 25- PHS 10

Bristol Eastern was led by Zoe Lowe with 15 aces and 5 kills, Emma Rodjenski with 6 kills, Sage Scarritt with 7 kills, Alyssa Adorno with 1 dig and 2 assists, and Hannah Podolak with 3 assists and 2 aces.

Plainville's record: 0-3
Bristol Eastern's record: 3-0

Bristol Eastern's next match will be on Thursday against Southington in the Lancerdome.
