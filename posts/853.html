A tree grows on the corner of Center St. and Bellevue Ave.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=853 Published on 2018-07-28 18:42:35
By David Fortier
Driving up Center St. on the way to the Farmers Market on North Main this morning, I noticed on the corner of Center St. and Bellevue Ave. that a new maple has been planted to replace the glorious maple that came down after one of the big storms earlier this year or even last year.
