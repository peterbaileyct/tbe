Christine Hackbarth

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8430 Published on 2021-10-06 18:24:14

Christine (Ouellette) Hackbarth, 61 of Bristol, passed away Oct. 4, at Ingraham Manor. She was the wife of James Hackbarth.

Christine was born on Dec. 21, 1959, in Bangor, Maine, the daughter of the late Bertrand and Diana (Peterson) Ouellette.

Beside her husband, she is survived by a daughter, April and her husband Scott Sirois of Bristol; six brothers Joseph Leonard, Philip, Ronald, Frankie, and Brian; four grandchildren Austin, Juistin, Brianna, and Brodie; plus several nieces and nephews.

She is pre-deceased by a son, Brian Ouellette and a sister, Carlene Ouellette.

Friends may call at Dunn Funeral Home 191 West St., Bristol, on Thursday at 11 a.m.
