BC softball a 15-run winner over Plainville on Monday afternoon

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=5239 Published on 2021-04-26 23:07:35

By Michael Letendre

BRISTOL - The Bristol Central softball squad continues its torrid start to the 2021 campaign and in a CCC South confrontation, the program from Plainville was simply blitzed by the Rams on Monday afternoon.

And in the end, Central etched up a huge 16-1 victory from the BC softball field.

The Rams are now 4-1 overall and have won two straight games since falling at Southington (9-6) on Friday.

But against the Blue Devils (0-2), the locals needed just five innings to seal the deal.

On the hill for Central, Alex Sciarretto dominated as she zipped up 13 strikeouts in the 15-run, mercy-ruled victory. 

And guess who had another home run? The answer is Gwen Torreso, as the senior has made those kind of hits a staple of her offensive arsenal.

She belted a round-tripper while teammate Janessa Bartell added three RBI to the till.
