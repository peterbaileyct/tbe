Little League registration runs through Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7527 Published on 2021-08-25 17:37:10

Registration for Bristol Little League's fall season will close Saturday, Aug. 28. The fall season runs from Sept. 10 through Oct. 24. Interested parties can register online by visiting www.bristolctll.com.

Fall baseball programs and fees follow:

  • Tee Ball (ages 4-6) ... $25
  • Minors (ages 5-9) ... $25
  • AAA (ages 8-11) ... $50
  • Majors (ages 9-12) ... $50
  • 50/70 (ages 11-13) ...$75
  • Juniors (ages 13-14) ... $125
  • Seniors (ages 15-16) ... $125
