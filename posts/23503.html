Bristol Police Department announces parking ban due to imminent snowfall

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=23503 Published on 2023-02-27 14:25:41

The Bristol Police Department announced in a press release on Monday afternoon that a city-wide public street ban will go into effect from 7 p.m. today, February 27, until 8 p.m. February 28 due to the anticipated snowfall.

Violations of the parking ban may result in vehicles towed and a $35 snow emergency ticket.
