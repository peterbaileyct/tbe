Little League holds opening ceremonies

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=5315 Published on 2021-05-01 17:02:51

Bristol Little League held its first opening ceremonies for a single league in three events at Edgewood School earlier today. Under blue skies and wearing masks participants tossed baseballs, gathered across the outfield for introductions, and heard from city officials, including Mayor Ellen Zoppo-Sassu. (Photos by R. Carter)
