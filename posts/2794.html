City resumes regular curbside trash collection after Storm Isaias

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2794 Published on 2020-08-24 11:12:44

Brush/branch collection by appointment only

Public Works operations return to normal beginning today, according to a release from the Mayor's office.

The brush/branch collection service will still be offered during the Yard Waste season, which runs through the first week of November, Public Works Director Ray Rogozinski said in the release.

Brush/branch collection services are by appointment only and are scheduled on Wednesdays. Residents whose rubbish and recycling is collected by the City of Bristol are eligible for this service.

For more information and to request a pickup access: http://ct-bristol2.civicplus.com/231/Brush-Branch-Pickup.

In addition, brush and branches, bundled per the requirements, along with other yard waste is still available for city residents at the Solid Waste Transfer Station with a residential permit.
