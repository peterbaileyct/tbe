McFadden funeral arrangements set

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7196 Published on 2021-08-14 17:42:39

Funeral arrangements for volunteer Burlington firefighter Colin McFadden have been finalized.

A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Thursday, Aug. 19, at 11 a.m. at St. Matthew Church, 120 Church Ave. Bristol.

Burial will follow in St. Joseph Cemetery, Bristol.

Calling hours will be held on Wednesday, Aug. 18, between 3 and 8 p.m. at Funk Funeral Home, 35 Bellevue Ave., Bristol.
