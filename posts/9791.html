Bristol overnight street parking ban begins Dec. 15

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9791 Published on 2021-12-07 23:23:15

The City of Bristol announced that beginning Wednesday, December 15, The Bristol police department will be enforcing the overnight parking ban on public streets.

As outlined in Bristol city ordinance 14-32, no vehicles may park on any city street between the hours of 2 a.m. and 6 a.m.

Parking tickets for overnight violation are $20.00. The fine doubles after 7 days and triples after 14 days.

Tickets can be paid over the phone (860-584-3061) with a credit/debit card or at the police department front desk with exact cash. Please do not send cash through the mail.

The overnight parking ban ends on March 15, 2022.
