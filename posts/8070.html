BE Volleyball runs record to 4-1 with straight set victory over Middletown

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8070 Published on 2021-09-18 16:39:07

The Bristol Eastern Volleyball team took to the road to face Middletown Friday night and came away with sweep of the Blue Dragons in 3 straight sets.

Hannah Webber led the Lancer offense with 8 kills and 2 aces. Lauren Bender matched Webber’s kills total with an additional 8 kills. And Abby Jones had 21 assists and 2 aces.

Middleton drops to 0-2 with the loss.

Eastern’s record is now 4-1.


Set scores:
Set 1: 25-13 BE
Set 2: 25-16 BE
Set 3: 25-16 BE
