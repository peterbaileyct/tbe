Bristol take-out food list supports local businesses

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=1515 Published on 2020-04-02 00:40:58

By David Fortier

The Bristol Edition has posted a Google Doc: Where to Find Take-Out Food in Bristol for local Bristol restaurants and eateries to share their information, including hours and specials, as well as instructions for pick-up and/or delivery.

Anyone who clicks on the link to the document here or above can update the list. The hope is that the list will grow to include all eateries offering take-out in Bristol.

Note: The document is adapted from one that has been circulating via The New Haven Independent in New Haven since mid-March. The original is the creation of Quinnipiac University political scientist and Community Foundation for Greater New Haven board chair Khalilah Brown Dean. The NIH ran a story about Dean and her list on 16 March.
