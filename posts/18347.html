Vigil in honor of fallen and wounded Police Officers to be held at Bristol Police Station tonight

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18347 Published on 2022-10-14 12:03:48

A vigil will be held tonight at the Bristol Police Department to honor fallen Officers Alex Hamzy and Sgt. Dustin Demonte and wounded Officer Alec Iurato.

The Salvation Army of Bristol Connecticut emergency canteen will be onsite, providing beverages, snacks and support for the public.

The vigil begins at 6:30 outside the Bristol Police Department, located at 131 North Main Street.
