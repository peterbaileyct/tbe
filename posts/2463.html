Floyd organizes Peaceful Protest at Norton Park, June 24

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2463 Published on 2020-06-23 21:00:33

Melina Floyd, BEHS Class of 2014, is planning her second Peaceful Protest Wednesday, June 24, from 5 to 8 p.m. at Norton Park in Plainville.

Floyd organized the protest that drew hundreds to Federal Hill Green earlier in June. A link to her interview with The Bristol Edition is available here.

As with the first protest, participants are being asked to wear a face mask, socially distance, and bring water and signs. In addition, the evening will begin with people who have gathered sharing their stories.
