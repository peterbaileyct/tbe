Barlow Street closed for utility work

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=14488 Published on 2022-06-30 07:45:00

Barlow Street will be closed from Terryville Road (Rte 72) to Pequabuck Street today for utility construction but will be open to local traffic.

Detours will be posted and the road will be reopened as soon as possible. It is anticipated the work will take one day.

Any questions should be directed to the Bristol Public Works Department at (860) 584-6125.
