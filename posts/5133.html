Eversource announces gas construction on Redstone Hill and surrounding area

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=5133 Published on 2021-04-15 10:17:20

Eversource has recently notified the City of Bristol’s Public Works Department that the Eversource Gas division will be expanding its natural gas distribution system in the Redstone Hill and Rogers Road area.

Beginning April 19, 2021 and continuing through approximately August 9, 2021, NPL Construction will be performing the work which will take place Monday through Friday and occasionally on Saturdays, between 7am to 4:30pm. The work is weather dependent and construction activities are subject to change.

Minor traffic delays will occur during construction, however roads will remain open for mail delivery, emergency vehicles, and local traffic at all times. Temporary “No Parking” signs will be posted as needed during construction.

Questions or concerns regarding this project should be directed to the Eversource Gas Construction Supervisor, Chris Tralli (860-302-6024) or Jesse Arsenault, NPL Construction Superintendent (203-673-8508)
