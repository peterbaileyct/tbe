Power outrages fluctuate according to Eversource outage map and region report

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2694 Published on 2020-08-09 19:57:11

Eversource's online outage map of Bristol and the complementary list of outages on a separate page (click on the Connecticut button and scroll down to Bristol) show 1,375 residences without power around 8 p.m. on Sunday evening, up from 1,337 the previous night at around 7 p.m. That leaves 4.6 percent of Bristol residences left without power.
