Brass and Wind Pops Concert brings out crowd

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=6259 Published on 2021-06-27 18:08:59

By David Fortier

An audience gathered in the shade of the mighty oaks on the perimeter of the Rockwell Park amphitheater Sunday afternoon to listen to a free performance by the Bristol Brass and Wind Ensemble featuring favorites from the American pop tradition, ranging from Dave Brubeck's "Take 5," The Wizard of Oz's, "Over the Rainbow" to a medley honoring veterans.
