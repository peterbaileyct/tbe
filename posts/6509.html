Memorial Boulevard Bridge Project- construction to close East and West bound lanes

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=6509 Published on 2021-07-16 16:20:37

The following news release was issued by the city of Bristol today (7/16/2021)

The City of Bristol began construction of the Memorial Boulevard Bridge
Project in early June.

“The project includes the rehabilitation of the Memorial Boulevard Bridge and is part of the State/Local bridge program,” said Mayor Ellen Zoppo-Sassu. “Up until now, one lane has been opened while we were waiting for the DOT to issue the final permits. However, effective Monday, July 19, both of the east and westbound lanes from Downs Street to East Street will no longer be accessible.”

“Detours are in place during the Memorial Boulevard Bridge replacement, said Councilman
Peter Kelley, a member of the Public Works Commission. “As a reminder, from Mellon Street
westbound, we are down to one lane as the retaining wall damage is completed.”

The City of Bristol is asking for the cooperation of motorists in avoiding the area for the duration of the project.  The project is scheduled to be completed by December of 2021.


Questions and/or comments may be directed to Raymond Rogozinski, PE, Director of Public
Works or Nancy Levesque, PE, City Engineer at 860-584-6125.
