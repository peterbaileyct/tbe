Local candidates list their endorsements

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2983 Published on 2020-10-16 19:32:23

Compiled by Jack Krampitz

The Bristol Edition asked the chairmen of the town committees, Republican Jeffrey Caggiano and Democrat Morris "Rippy" Patton, respectively, to provide us with a list of endorsements their candidates had gained for the upcoming elections.

Either the chairman or the candidates replied. The list indicates who responded. In the case of independent candidates, the candidates were emailed directly. Only organizational, and not personal, endorsements were included. Here is what we received.
