Anyone can participate in community audit

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2569 Published on 2020-07-18 17:06:55

During August, the Bristol Public Works Dept. is conducting a community audit with the goal of making the city more pedestrian friendly.

Walkers, bikers and joggers in neighborhoods across the city can report any issues with cross walks, crossing signals, and sidewalks on a Walk Audit Sheet at the end of the month.

The Walk Audit Sheet can be downloaded by clicking here. After it is completed, the document can be scanned and emailed to publicworks@bristolct.gov.

A map of neighborhood districts is available here.

For more information, contact the Bristol Public Works Department at (860) 584-6103.
