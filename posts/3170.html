Farmers to families food distribution set for today

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=3170 Published on 2020-10-30 08:40:33

The Mayor's office has announced that the Farmers to Families Produce Distribution program will take place this Friday, October 30th. Distribution
will take place from 3pm-5:30pm at the new location--Lake Compounce Parking Lot. (185 Enterprise Drive).

The times are subject to change based on the arrival of the truck. To get updated information for these weekly deliveries, please call 860-582-9559.

The food boxes will include not only fresh produce, but also meat and dairy products. The program is free and is not income-based.
