Rockwell Park Concert Series begins on Tuesday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=6083 Published on 2021-06-21 18:56:40

The Rockwell Park Summer Concert Series begins this Tuesday night.

This week's concert is Darik and the Funbags. The West End Association has arranged for Adelaide Punkin to be the opening act.

They have also arranged for the Sultan Kabob food truck to be there at 5:00 PM, so you will be able to purchase food during the entertainment.

Adelaide Punkin will hit the stage at 5:45 and Darik and the Funbags will begin at 6:30.


Bring a blanket, friend, neighbor, and enjoy the beautiful Rockwell Park amphitheater. 

The West End Association is the presenting sponsor of the Summer Music Concert series coordinated by the Bristol Park & Recreation Department.
