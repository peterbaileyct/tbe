Bristol Police arrest suspect on Saturday on multiple drug charges

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8538 Published on 2021-10-12 20:59:55

Bristol police announced that on Saturday, October 9, while on routine patrol, Bristol patrol officers located Kaylon Porcher, age 25, of New Britain, sitting in a parked car on Summer Street.

Officers were aware of an active arrest warrant for Porcher for illegal possession of a firearm. During a search incident to arrest, police located and seized 18 grams of suspected cocaine, 18 Oxycodone pills, 6 Adderall pills, and over $1,800 in suspected drug proceeds.

Porcher was charged with possession of a controlled substance, possession with intent to sell narcotics, and interfering with police. He posted $410,000 bond and was issued a court date of October 18 at New Britain court.
