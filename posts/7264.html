Tag Sales- A New TBE Service

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7264 Published on 2021-08-16 12:46:33

The Bristol Edition is now offering a free service to our readers. If you would like to have an announcement of a future tag sale, just send an email to jmkrampitz@bristoledition.org.

When you give us your information, please put it into the following format:

Date of sale

Location

Time

Featured items (optional)

To announce a tag sale any week, we will need your info by the end of the day on Tuesday of that week. We will then publish the tag sale notice on Wednesday and Friday.
