Bristol Eastern volleyball team runs record to 7-3 with a three set sweep of Conard

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=34919 Published on 2023-10-04 14:15:19

If anyone was worried about a Bristol Eastern letdown after their emotional come-from-behind victory over Bristol Central last week, well the only thing we can tell you is - relax.

The Lancers kept their momentum going with a straight sets triumph over Conard Tuesday night.

The set scores for the match were 25-21, 25-17, and 25-23.

Eastern was paced by Andrea Martir-Perez, who had 16 digs and 1 assist, and Adrianna Reay with 21 kills, an ace and 9 digs. Gabby Caucci added 11 kills, an ace and 8 digs. And Naseem Hennouch had an incredible 32 assists along with 6 digs.

The Lancers are home on Thursday for a 5:45 contest against a tough E.O. Smith squad.

Records:
Bristol Eastern 7-3
Conard 4-5
