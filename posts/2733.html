City announces fall paving program

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2733 Published on 2020-08-11 12:42:49

Beginning today, Aug. 11, the following streets will be milled and then resurfaced as part of the city's fall paving program:

  • North Main St. (School St to Laurel) thru Andrews St.
  • Mines Rd. (Stafford to Arrowhead) thru Evelyn Rd.
  • Poitras Road (Village to Redstone) thru Aldbourne Dr.
  • Ashover Ln. thru York St.
  • Pennwood Pl. thru Joseph St.
  • Allen St. thru Litchfield Ln.
  • Danbury Ln. thru Goshen Ct.
  • Ingraham Pl. thru Nestico Ct.
  • Reilly Pl. thru Ingraham St.
  • Tuttle St. thru Howard Ave.
  • Barnum Rd. thru Bishop St.
  • Pheasant Run Rd. thru Kozani St.
  • Quail Hollow Ln.

Traffic may be impeded at times, but roads will remain open for all other purposes, according a release from the city. Temporary "no parking" signs will be posted. The city will repair driveway aprons once construction is completed.

Questions or concerns about these projects should be directed to the Public Works Engineering Division at 584-6125.
