"Ten Minutes" with Andrew Wood

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2077 Published on 2020-06-04 16:12:52

Hosted by David Fortier

This week, "Ten Minutes" features Andrew Wood, an educator and family friend. Andrew and I talked Sunday, May 31, over the phone. As with most educators, he is working from home. In Wood's case, however, he is working with K-5 students, and he is teaching Physical Education, an activity with its own unique challenges.
