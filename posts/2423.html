Bell ringing marks 100 days

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2423 Published on 2020-06-28 20:40:41

Bristol marked 100 days since the March 20 Covid-19 shutdown with bell ringing at churches throughout the city on Saturday, June 20, at 4 p.m. Members of different faith communities gathered for an interfaith service in the parking area by St. Gregory Church at 4:30 p.m.
