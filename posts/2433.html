Farmers Market opens with social distancing, draws a crowd

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2433 Published on 2020-06-22 11:06:59

The Farmers Market opened for its season Saturday, June 20, drawing a large and steady crowd to its location across from City Hall. Shoppers, and introducing new social distancing rules, direction signs and recommendations for face masks. The market season ends in October.
