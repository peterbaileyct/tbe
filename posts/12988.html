Green Hills School closed for a short time Friday morning

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12988 Published on 2022-05-06 13:52:10

Bristol police announced that Green Hills School was closed for a brief time Friday morning ”out of an abundance of caution based on suspicious writing found on a wall of the school.”

By 10:30 a.m. the school was re-opened and the day proceeded normally.

The police stated that no one at the school was ever in danger during the incident.
