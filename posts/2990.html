Eastern defeats Lewis Mills in volleyball

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2990 Published on 2020-10-13 22:13:33

The Lancer volleyball team made quick work of Lewis Mills Tuesday afternoon, winning in three straight sets. The set scores were:
Set 1: 25-15
Set 2: 25-16
Set 3: 25-20

Eastern was led by Emma Rodjenski with 9 kills, Alyssa Adorno with 3 aces, and Abby Jones with 3 assists.

For Lewis Mills, Makenzie Jones had 7 kills and Madison Sadecki had 12 digs.

Bristol Eastern is now 5-0.
Lewis Mills drops to 1-4.
