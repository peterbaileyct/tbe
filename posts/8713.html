Evening on the Hill at the Bristol Historical Society

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8713 Published on 2021-10-22 08:50:53

The Bristol Historical Society will be abuzz tomorrow night because they are holding their annual Evening on the Hill Auction.

The event begins at 5:30 p.m. with an auction preview, with food, wine, and soft drinks. The Live Auction follows at 7 p.m. with auctioneer Paul Stansel.

“We have the usual assortment of furniture, antiques, collectibles, and a few quirky items. We have many dolls, including a donation from a doll collector featuring a lot Madame Alexander Dolls, which are very collectible,” said BHS president Maya Bringe. “This event is a wonderful way of supporting the organization.”

Tickets are $12 in advance, $15 at the door, and in the interest of safety, everyone attending must wear a mask.

For more information or to buy tickets, call the historical society at 860-583-6309 or email president@bristolhistoricalsociety.org.  The Bristol Historical Society is located at 98 Summer St.

“Even if you don’t walk away with anything, you will have a great time because it is a really good show,” Bringe remarked.
