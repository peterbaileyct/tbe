SNOW EMERGENCY PARKING BAN

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=4346 Published on 2021-02-08 15:33:20

There will be a snow emergency parking ban in effect starting on Tuesday, February 9, 2021, from 2:00 a.m.-9:00 p.m.

Vehicles parked on city streets during the parking ban may be towed and tagged to allow for snow clearing operations.
