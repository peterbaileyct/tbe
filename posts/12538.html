Community Clean Up Day this Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12538 Published on 2022-04-19 15:08:58

The City of Bristol is holding a Clean Up Day this Saturday from 8:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.

Everyone is encouraged to pitch in to help clean specific areas of town or to spruce up your own street or neighborhood.

Supplies for the clean can be picked up at Park Department headquarters at 51 High Street from 8:00 to 10:00 a.m. on Saturday.
