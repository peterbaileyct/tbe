Greek festival today at St. Demetrios

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=14162 Published on 2022-06-19 07:00:00

The Zorba 2022 Festival runs from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m. today at St. Demetrios Church on Brightwood Rd. The annual festival features Greek food, including baklava, gyros, souvlaki, lamb shanks, spanakopita, among others. Proceeds from the festival go directly to church needs.
