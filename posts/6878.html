Concert series features rhythm and soul band

Summer concert series at Rockwell Park Amphitheater features Soul Sound Revue with Jim Pelletier at 6:30 p.m. Opening act starts at 6 p.m.

Heavenly Smoke Barbeque will be on the premises to provide food and drink.

Participants are encouraged to bring their own chairs, blankets, family and friends for the free concert.

The last concert in this year's program is Aug. 10, featuring Latanya Farrell.

The series is sponsored by Bristol Parks, Recreation, Community and Youth Service in cooperation with the West End Association, which arranges the food.
