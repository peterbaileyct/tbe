Eastern Volleyball sweeps past Berlin Monday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8679 Published on 2021-10-19 19:49:46

The Bristol Eastern volleyball team defeated Berlin in straight sets Monday night in the Lancer gym.


The Set scores were:
Set 1: 25-20
Set 2: 25-20
Set 3: 25-17

Berlin’s record drops to 5-12

Bristol Eastern’s record improves to 12-3.

Julia Paul led Berlin with 5 kills and 3 blocks.


Bristol Eastern had great efforts from Emily Valentine, with 3 aces and 6 digs, Alex Shorette with 2 aces, 5 kills and 2 digs, and Lilli Lowe with 2 kills and 1 block.

The Lancers next play at Conard Wednesday at 5:00.
