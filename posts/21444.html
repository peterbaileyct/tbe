Taste of Bristol tickets now available

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21444 Published on 2023-01-03 08:21:12

The West End Association has announced that they now have Taste of Bristol tickets available for purchase for the March/April 2023 promotion. The tickets make it possible for people to receive discounts on orders at dozens of Bristol restaurants.

Individual tickets can be bought at several locations around town.

The Taste of Bristol fundraiser helps the West End Association as they prepare for the Rockwell Park Summer Festival.
