Jane Cucuel, 93

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=14311 Published on 2022-06-25 07:00:00

Jane (Hurlburt) Cucuel, 93, of Bristol, widow of Norman A. Cucuel, died on Thursday, June 23, at her home. Jane was born in Bristol on Jan. 13, 1929, and was the only child of the late Roy and Myra (Smith) Hurlburt. A lifelong Bristol resident, she worked for Bristol Savings Bank for several years and was a member of the Saybrook Colonial Founders Association and First Congregational Church of Bristol. Jane is survived by a son, two grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren. Funeral and burial in West Cemetery, Bristol, will be private and at the convenience of the family. Please visit Jane’s memorial web-site at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
