Little League softball tournaments begin next week

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=6119 Published on 2021-06-23 09:55:38

Bristol’s Little League softball district tournament season will begin next week.

 Bristol’s 12 year old team will play a best of three series with Wallingford from Dunn Sports Complex in Meriden 6/28-6/30.  

The Junior League softball district tournament will be hosted in Bristol at Rockwell Park 6/28-7/2.  Bristol is currently scheduled to play Farmington on 6/28.  The winner will play Meriden the following day.  District championship will be played on July 1st (July 2nd, if nec)

The 10 year old district softball team will participate the in the district tournament hosted by Wallingford 7/6-7/10.

All double elimination games are currently scheduled to start at 6pm.  

The winner of each of these tournaments will play in the sectional tournament.  Teams that advance beyond the sectional round will play in the state tournament.
