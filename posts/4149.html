Winter scholastic sports schedule released on the CIAC website

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=4149 Published on 2021-01-21 10:52:37

By Michael Letendre

The CIAC finally announced the winter scholastic sports schedule for boys and girls basketball and boys swimming on Wednesday as practices are underway for the programs.

Those winter slates are going to be extremely local with the Bristol squads competing against the usual teams of the CCC South division with a drizzling of games against opponents from the North and West.

However, the squads from Bristol will not be traveling across the Connecticut River for the first time ever over its scholastic history due to the effects of COVID-19.

All basketball games, for both boys and girls, will be played on the same dates and for now, the Central/Eastern contests take place on February 12 and then again on March 11 at the exact same time.

And those hardwood contests all commence at 6:45 p.m. and each school plays six games at home and another six on the road.

For the Bristol Central boys team, who went 17-5 overall last season, the program has an extremely busy first week of action.

On Tuesday, February 9, the Rams open up at home from the Charles C. Marsh Gymnasium against Avon while squaring off against city rival Bristol Eastern on the road three days later.

Over a three game stretch in mid-March, Central is home for Plainville (March 8), Eastern (March 11), and New Britain (March 15) before tangling with Lewis Mills (March 18) to close out the regular season.

The Lancers (6-14 in 2019-20), drew a tough Farmington squad to commence the season on February 9 from the Thomas M. Monahan Gymnasium.

Eastern plays three of its first four games at home and then finishes the campaign on March 18 against New Britain.

On the girls end, Bristol Central (6-15 overall) and Bristol Eastern (18-6) both qualified for postseason play in the Class L bracket and are looking to do some damage along the way of the shortened season.

Central opens on the road against Avon on February 9 and then makes its home debut on February 12 versus Eastern in a city series bout.

The Rams also have home dates against Plainville (February 19), New Britain (February 23), Farmington (March 1), Southington (March 4) and Lewis Mills (March 18).

And then the Lancers storm the road to open the season against Farmington (March 9) and Central before playing against Plainville on February 16 from the Thomas M. Monahan Gymnasium.

From February 23 through March 11, Eastern plays five out of six contests from home with a March 11 date at Central in the mix.

The plan is to have some sort of postseason tournament at the conclusion of the regular season but there will not be any official state tournament championship for the second straight year.

On the boys swimming front, the squad will once again be comprised of all three schools in town (as of Wednesday night, the CIAC website said that all home meets will take place from the Bristol Eastern front field instead of the Dennis Malone Aquatic Center. Swamp swimming at its finest…) with seven total meets on the docket.

All meets commence at 3:45 p.m. for all swimming contests.

Middletown will be as far as the Bristol trio travels with road dates at Avon and Meriden mixed in.

Bristol opens the 2021 swimming season at home versus Farmington on Wednesday, February 10.
