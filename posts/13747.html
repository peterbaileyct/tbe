BC Softball game venue changed to Casey Field today

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=13747 Published on 2022-06-02 11:30:46

By Michael Letendre

BRISTOL - Today's second round Class LL softball game between Bristol Central and Cheshire will now commence from Casey Field on Middle Street at 3:15 p.m.

Central's softball field would not be ready in time for the showdown as the recent rain has made the facility unplayable for the contest.
