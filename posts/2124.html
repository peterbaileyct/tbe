Covid cases rise slightly

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2124 Published on 2020-06-07 20:31:59

As of Saturday, June 6, the state of Connecticut coronavirus website reported 576 confirmed cases in Bristol, up from 525 the previous week, with 71 deaths up from 68.

The case rate increased from 875 to 959 per 100,000. The numbers of tests completed tested rose from 2,420 to 5,344.

The number of total cases in the state increased to 43,968, up from 41,288, with 4,071 deaths up from 3803. 300,281 tests have been completed, up from 229,769.
