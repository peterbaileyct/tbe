TBE Sports Sunday: Bristol athletes earn CCC South All-Conference honors

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=6231 Published on 2021-06-27 14:32:41

By Michael Letendre 

The Central Connecticut Conference website has recently updated its 2021 spring All-Conference teams and in terms of the CCC South, listings for baseball, softball, boys and girls lacrosse – along with boys tennis.

Here’s a look at all of Bristol’s CCC South, All-Conference honorees: 

CCC South All-Conference Selections 

2021 Baseball All Conference 

Bristol Central - JT Clark, Mike Lorenzetti, Sean Wininger 

Bristol Eastern - Andrew Lozier 

2021 Softball All Conference 

Bristol Central - Gwen Torreso, Alex Sciarretto, Kaley Laird

Bristol Eastern - Jasmine Perez, Janessa Gonzalez, Alison Tellier 

2021 Boys Lacrosse All Conference 

Bristol – Andrew Spina, Jack Stavens, Jeffrey Tallion 

2021 Girls Lacrosse All Conference 

Bristol Central - Lily Hansen, Sadye Kowalczyk, Jenna Lowe, Lexi Michaud, Alyah Rodriguez- Warren 

Bristol Eastern - Ciara Collins, Kate Kozikowski, Emma Stavens

2021 Boys Track All Conference 

Bristol Central – Cassias Breland, Xavier Browne, Noah Conrod, Nate DeAngelo, Austin Freve, Nicolas Jones, Ramon Peters, Mitchell Peterson, Francis Porrini, Victor Rosa, Jakob Salinas 

Bristol Eastern – Carter Harris, Dante Marciano, Rashad Williams 

2021 Boys Tennis All Conference 

Bristol Central - Beckett Hennessey

Bristol Eastern - Nolan Abitabilo, Brandon Greger, Nathaniel Woodford
