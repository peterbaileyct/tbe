Rinette Ouellette

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8007 Published on 2021-09-16 17:52:54

Rinette (Raymond) Ouellette, 87, of Bristol passed away Tuesday, Sept. 14, at The Reservoir in West Hartford. She was the widow of Ernest J. Ouellette.

She was born in Fort Kent, Maine, the daughter of the late Baptist and Alvina (Bard) Raymond. 

She worked as a office manager 25 years for Plainville Water Department and retired in 1988. She loved to garden and will be missed by all.

She is survived by a son, Gary R. Ouellette and his wife Linda of Bristol and a daughter, Beverly Woodworth and her husband Tim of Bristol. A brother, Joseph A. Raymond of Virginia. three grandchildren and  two great grandchildren plus several nieces and nephews.

A Mass of Christian Burial will be held at 10 a.m. on Friday, Sept. 17, at St. Matthew Church.

Burial will follow in St. Joseph Cemetery. Dunn Funeral Home 191 West St. Bristol, is in charge of the arrangements.  Visit  WWW.Dunnfh.com for more information.
