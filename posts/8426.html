BC Girls Swimming posts big win over Plainville

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8426 Published on 2021-10-06 09:27:45

The Bristol Central girls swim and dive team put together another solid effort in a 90 - 72 victory over Plainville.

First place finishers for the Lady Rams were Isabella Cordero in the 200 IM, Katelyn Holleran in the 50 free and 500 free. Nyomi Cordero also placed first in the 100 fly and 100 backstroke and Isabel Paolino was victorious in the 100 breast. 

Christina Tomcak took a first in diving and also qualified for states in diving.

Also taking first place was the Central 200 Medley relay team  (I. Cordeo, Paolino, Madison Pikiell and Tomcak)

The lady Rams had 18 personal best times. 

Central’s next meet is on Thursday evening a Tri meet against Farmington and Eastern.
