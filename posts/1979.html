"Ten Minutes" with Reinhart Walker

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=1979 Published on 2020-05-28 14:35:19

Hosted by David Fortier

Reinhart Walker teaches math at Bristol Central High School. A lifelong Bristol resident, Walker is a member of the 1976 Forestville Little League All-Star team that made it all the way to Williamsport and has served on the Bristol Board of Education. He is a family friend. At the moment, like many of us, he is sheltering at home with his family.

David Fortier is publisher of TBE. He teaches high school English and is a member of the Connecticut Education Association. He serves as state director of the Journalism Education Association. His wife, Mary, is a city council member who is running for state senator in the 31st District.
