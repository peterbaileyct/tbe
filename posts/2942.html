Plainville Boys best Bristol Eastern in soccer

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2942 Published on 2020-10-06 23:00:00

Plainville (3-0) beat Bristol Eastern (0-1-1) Tuesday afternoon, 1-0, behind a hustle goal by Michael Ouelette at 28:15 of the first half.  Lancer goalie Nate Diloreto got a hand on Ouelette's shot, but it just made it into the corner. 

Diloretto made 6 saves for BE, while Plainville's Nathan Masco had 8. 

Eastern had many opportunities, especially in the second half, but shots were wide of the net  or miskicked in front of the goal.   

Lancer backs Luke Hammer, Adam Borry, Andrew Lozier, and Brandon Greger kept Plainville at bay, except for the one goal as Eastern controlled much of the play in the second half.   

Eastern hosts Southington on Friday afternoon.
