Bristol RTC announces slate for 2021 municipal elections

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7269 Published on 2021-08-17 08:47:19

The Bristol Republican Town Committee recently endorsed its candidates for the upcoming municipal election on Tuesday, Nov. 2. 

The following is their roster of candidates:

For the Bristol Assessment Board of Appeals--

Mary Alford and Erick Rosengren.

For the Board of Education--

Todd Sturgeon, Eric Carlson and Dante Tagariello.

For City Council, District 1--

Jolene Lusitani and Sebastien Panioto.

For City Council, District 2--

Jacqueline Olsen and Sue Parent Tyler. 

For City Council, District 3--

Cheryl Thibeault and Andre Howe.

For Mayor--

Jeff Caggiano.
