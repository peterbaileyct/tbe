DTC chair Rippy Patton discusses this year's municipal campaign

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8371 Published on 2021-10-03 14:44:34

TBE's David Fortier sits down with Democratic Town Committee chair Rippy Patton to this year's municipal election, which takes place Nov. 2. TBE has invited Republican Town Committee chair Rich Kilby to have a similar discussion. A date for the sit down has yet to be determined. The conversation with Patton took place Saturday, Oct. 25.
