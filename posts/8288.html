BC Girls Swim Team dunks East Hartford, 92-73

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8288 Published on 2021-09-29 13:32:27

The Bristol Central Girls Swim & Dive team posted a convincing win against East Hartford Tuesday afternoon. Multiple girls took firsts in the meet, and 23 swimmers posted their best times of the season.

First place finishers for the Rams were Katelyn Holleran in the 200 free, Nyomi Cordero in the 50 free, Christina Tomcak in diving, Madison Pikiell in the 100 free, Isabella Cordero in the 100 back, and Isabel Paolino in the 100 breast.

Naomi Cordero, Rayiah Reinhard, Holleran and Isabella Cordero also captured a first in the 400 free relay.

Bristol Central improved to 2-1 with the victory.
