Leaf bag collection begins Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2980 Published on 2020-10-12 20:37:03

The 2020 Fall Leaf Bag Collection will begin Tuesday, Oct. 13, and run through Friday, Nov. 20, weather-permitting, according a notice on the city's website. Each area will be collected from once a week.Â 

The notice instructs residents to bring their leaf bags to the curbside, where they can be left until collected.

There is no maximum limit to leaf bag collection at a residence, but only brown, recyclable, paper leaf bags will be included.

Leaf bags containing dirt, sand, rocks, sod or materials other than leaves will not be collected. Loose leaf piles will not be collected.
