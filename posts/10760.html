American Clock and Watch Museum offers free entry to Bristol health care workers in February

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10760 Published on 2022-01-20 16:54:23

Colleen Nicastro, Director of Interpretation at the American Clock & Watch Museum, has issued the following invitation to Bristol’s health care workers.


“Do you work at a healthcare facility or doctor’s office located in the All Heart city of Bristol? If you do, the American Clock & Watch Museum is offering you one FREE adult admission to the museum when you visit during the month of February,” said Nicastro.
“The process is simple. Just pop by the museum any time Wednesday through Sunday from 10:00-5:00, show your work ID to the admission staff, and you will be admitted free of charge. It’s our way of thanking those people in our city who work so hard to keep things ticking!”
