Woman shot at Huntington Woods Monday afternoon

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7635 Published on 2021-08-31 19:50:27

On Monday, August 30, 2021 at approximately 2:00 p.m. Bristol police officers responded to Huntington Woods apartments at 200 Blakeslee Street for a report that a female had been shot.

When officers arrived, they found a 41 year old female suffering from a gunshot wound in an apartment.

EMS was called to the scene and the female was transported to an area hospital, where she remains in serious but stable condition.

A preliminary investigation determined the incident appears to be isolated and there is no danger to the public.

The incident is being investigated by the Bristol Police Criminal Investigation Division. Anyone with information is asked to call Detective Dauphinais at (860) 314-4566.
