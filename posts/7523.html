Blood drive at DoubleTree today

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7523 Published on 2021-08-25 13:19:05

The American Red Cross and American Legion Post 209 is holding a blood drive today from 1 to 6 p.m. at DoubleTree by Hilton of Bristol at 42 Century Dr.

To schedule an appointment, call 1-800-Red-Cross (1-800-733-2767) or sign up online at www.redcrossblood.org: use sponsor code BristolCT.
