TBE bulletin

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17008 Published on 2022-09-12 21:27:21

A caller has informed TBE of a high speed police chase on Farmington Avenue that included reports of gun fire at around 6:45 p.m. Several Bristol Police cars were in pursuit. Most of the avenue was shut down. Shots were fired in the vicinity of Walgreens and Stop and Shop.

Postings can be seen on Bristol Talks that include eyewitness reports.
