Farmers to Families food distribution set Friday, Aug. 7, across from City Hall

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2628 Published on 2020-08-04 18:47:22

The next Farmers to Families food distribution will be this Friday, Aug. 7 from noon to 2 p.m. in the parking lot across from City Hall.

Participants are being asked to to enter the parking lot on North Main St., drive to one of food stations, where a volunteer will load a food box in the back seat or the trunk of the vehicle, and exit onto Hope St.

The food distribution program is sponsored by the Mayor’s Office and the United Way. The program underwritten by the United States Department of Agriculture.

For more information or to volunteer for the Farmers to Families program, or to volunteer, please call the Mayor’s Office at 860-584-6250, the Mayor’s Office Facebook page or the News Flash section of the City’s website: www.bristolct.gov.
