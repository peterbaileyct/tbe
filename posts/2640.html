Trees come down and power outrages across the city

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2640 Published on 2020-08-04 19:59:21

Across the city, Storm Isaias brought down trees and caused power outrages. On Goodwin St., the majestic oak tree in front of the St. Joseph Parish Center toppled over, covering the front lawn and tumbling out to the street.
