BC Girls Swim team loses close meet at Middletown

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8675 Published on 2021-10-19 19:23:49

The Bristol Central girls swim team dropped a close 92-86 decision at Middletown on Friday afternoon.

The Rams still achieved multiple personal best times, including Nyomi Cordero in the 200 Free, Hannah Krueger in the 200 IM, Christina Tomcak, Alayna Lawson, and Meadow Wells in the 50 free. Wells also earned her best score in Diving, as did Rayiah Reinhard. Reinhard had her best time in the 500 free. Isabella Cordero and Emely Sosa had personal bests in the 100 Back. Finally, Madison Field had a best in the 100 Breast.  

Highighted events included the 200 Medley Relay with BC taking 1 and 2nd. 

In the 200 IM, BC outscored Middletown 9 – 7.

The Rams Divers took 1st and 2nd, with Rayiah Reinhard in first.

First place finishers also included I. Cordero, N. Cordero, Madison Pikiell, and Katelyn Holleran in 200 Medley Relay.

Central’s next meet is next Tuesday, 10/26 @ 5:00, Senior Night against Eastern.
