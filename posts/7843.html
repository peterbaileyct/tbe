Bristol Eastern Volleyball opens season with loss at South Windsor

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7843 Published on 2021-09-10 11:41:58

The Bristol Eastern volleyball team opened the 2021 season with 3-1 loss at South Windsor on Thursday night.

South Windsor got off to a quick start by winning the first set, 25-14.

Eastern countered quickly with a 25-20 second set decision.

The Bobcats then ran off the final two sets by scores of 25-18 and 25-22.

The Lancers (0-1) were led by Hannah Webber with 18 kills and Lilli Lowe with 7 kills.
