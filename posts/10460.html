City to distribute at-home test kits Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10460 Published on 2022-01-07 17:19:32

The City of Bristol will distribute at-home COVID test kids, along with N95 masks, at Firehouse #4 on Vincent P. Kelly Rd, from 8:30 to 11 a.m. on Saturday, Jan. 8, it was announced in a press release.

The iHealth at-home tests and N95 masks have been made available from the State of Connecticut and are the second allocation, recently. The exact number of tests and masks was not included in the release, but one kit and a mask will be allotted to anyone with a Bristol ID.

Please follow the posted signs to reach Firehouse #4 by going down Middle St. (Rte. 229) to Cross Street, turn right onto Lake Avenue, and then right onto to Vincent P. Kelly Rd.
