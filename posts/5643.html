Bristol American Legion Baseball tryouts announced

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=5643 Published on 2021-05-23 12:30:14

The Bristol Legion Baseball Program has scheduled their upcoming tryouts and practices, starting on Saturday, May 29.

Tryouts/practices will commence from Clem J. Roy Field from the campus of Bristol Eastern High School - starting at 1 p.m.

Practices will continue each day until season opens on June 10.

Post 2 will field two teams this season and will go back to the familiar format of 17U (Juniors) and 19U (Seniors) teams.

Candidates must first register in advance of the May 29 tryout at ctlegionbball.com and fill out Form 2 registration.  

New players to the Bristol program must bring their original birth certificates (not a copy) on the first day of practice/tryouts.

Players for the Bristol squad must reside from the following towns/cities: Bristol, Terryville, Plymouth, Thomaston, Wolcott or Waterbury (by zip code only).

This coming season, Bristol has scheduled over 50 games and expect a busy campaign for both the junior and senior teams from Post 2.

For more information, please contact coaches Jerry LaPenta (860) 995-6296 or Bill Mason (203) 885-3842.
