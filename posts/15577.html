Lt. Gov. Susan Bysiewicz has issued a written as well as a video endorsement of two local candidates running for the state house of representatives.

Democrats Andrew Rasmussen-Tuller and Mary Fortier, running for seats in state house Districts 77 and 79, respectively, received the endorsements recently.
