Flooding near Page Park and Bristol Eastern

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=25233 Published on 2023-04-23 15:05:09

A brook near the entrance to Page Park on King Street overflowed early Sunday afternoon, causing minor flooding at the traffic light at the entrances to Page Park and Bristol Eastern.

Residents are advised to use caution near the entrance to Page Park and Bristol Eastern High School and areas prone to significant street flooding.
