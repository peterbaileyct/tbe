Farmers Market opens Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=5956 Published on 2021-06-13 14:24:04

BRISTOL  – The Bristol Farmer’s Market will kick off its 2021 season by adding two new vendors to this year’s roster.

Opening day is on Saturday, June 19 from 10 a.m. until 1 p.m. at Centre Square, and market days will follow every Saturday until Oct. 30. 

Better Half Brewing and 3rd Day Nutraceuticals will be the newest vendors paticipating this year – joining the 15 other regular vendors.

There will be two food trucks – Dude’s Donuts and Heavenly Smoke BBQ.

Weekly vendors will include:

  • Amazing Love Farm
  • Better Baking by Beth
  • Cafe Real
  • Continuum Distilling
  • Eaton Farm
  • Firefly Hollow Brewing
  • Green Acres Farm
  • Gresczyk Farms
  • Nature View Farm
  • Oma’s Pride
  • Perkins Pure Maple Sugar House
  • Ridge Runner Soap Company
  • Tonn’s Orchard/Marketplace
  • Twin Pines Farm
  • West’s Side Jerky
