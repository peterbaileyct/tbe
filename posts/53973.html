Public Works Department extends hours for leaf disposal at the transfer station

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53973 Published on 2024-11-15 11:26:24

The Bristol Public’s Works Department has announced that the Transfer Station will have extended hours for leaf drop-off only, on Saturday, November 16th and Saturday, November 30th from 1:00 p.m. to 2:45 p.m.. 

The Transfer Station will not be open for any other activities.
