Bristol Hospital temporarily suspends ER Visitation

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10062 Published on 2021-12-28 07:09:29

Bristol Hospital has announced that it will temporarily suspend visitation for patients in its Emergency Room as a means to protect both patients and staff. Hospital personnel will continually reassess the situation and will make an announcement of when the ER will allow visitors. 

There is currently no timetable on when visitation will resume.
