Fatal motorcycle accident on Peck Lane

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=6688 Published on 2021-07-23 07:20:36

Bristol Police reported the following accident yesterday.

On 7/21/21 around 5:46 P.M. a motor vehicle accident occurred on Peck Lane at its intersection with Miller Road within the City of Bristol.

This accident involved a motorcycle and another vehicle traveling in opposing lanes on Peck Lane. The motorcycle and vehicle collided within the intersection of Peck Lane and Miller Road.

The motorcyclist was ejected from the motorcycle and suffered life threatening injuries.

Officers arrived on scene, treating the motorcyclist for his injuries, and Life Star transported the motorcyclist to Hartford Hospital.

The motorcyclist, later identified as Michael A. Genovese Jr. (D.O.B. 5/13/85), was pronounced dead a short time after arrival to the hospital.

The Bristol Police Serious Traffic Accident Reconstruction Team (S.T.A.R.T) was called to investigate the accident.

This accident remains under investigation and the Bristol Police Department is requesting that anyone who may have witnessed this collision contact Officer Ryan Kosiba who is the investigating officer for this case. Officer Kosiba can be contacted at 860-584-3035.
