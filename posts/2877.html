Latest COVID stats from the state

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=2877 Published on 2020-09-20 19:00:50

On Friday, the state of Connecticut coronavirus website reported 710 confirmed cases and 72 total confirmed deaths in Bristol.

At the same time, the number of confirmed cases in Connecticut totaled 55,527 confirmed cases and 4,492 deaths.

Also, the state has reported 1,419,839 COVID-19 PCR tests.
