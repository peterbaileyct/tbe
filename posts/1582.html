A new TBE podcast, "10 Minutes," features conversation with Mayor Ellen Zoppo-Sassu

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=1582 Published on 2020-04-17 17:37:06

With David Fortier

The Bristol Edition premieres its new podcast, "10 Minutes" with this brief interview featuring Mayor Ellen Zoppo-Sassu. The conversation took place yesterday, April 16, over the phone, between meetings. The goal was to stick to 10 minutes but went a few minutes longer with addition of an intro and some music.
