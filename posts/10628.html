Part of Middle St. to be closed today from 10 a.m. to noon.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10628 Published on 2022-01-14 06:26:28

The Bristol Police Department announced that on Friday, January 14, 2022, tie Police Department’s Serious Traffic Accident Reconstruction Team will be collecting evidence from a motor vehicle crash scene on Middle Street.  

Middle Street will be closed for both north and southbound traffic between Lake Avenue and West Gate from 10:00 A.M to 12:00 P.M.  Commuters can utilize Battisto Road and Lake Avenue to bypass the road closure.
