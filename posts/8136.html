Sonia M. Lavigne

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=8136 Published on 2021-09-21 19:01:22

Sonia M. (St-Germain) Lavigne, 74, of Bristol passed away, Sept. 18, at her home. She was the wife of William E. Lavigne.

She was born on Jan. 24, 1947, the daughter of the late Wilfred and Pearl (Morisette) St-Germain.

She worked for several manufacturing companies in the area and retired in 2019.

Besides her husband she is survived by two sons, William Thomas and his wife Lisa and Troy Joseph Lavigne and his companion Kelley Kilduff, both from Bristol; three sisters, Denise Jansen, Lorraine Frederick and Linda St-Germain; and seven grandchildren, Owen, Eliott, Amelia, Jaiden, Lily Rose, Mikayln and Kaitlyn.

She is pre-deceased by a brother, Daniel St-Germain.

A Memorial Mass will be held on Saturday, Sept. 25, at 10 a.m. at St. Gregory Church, Bristol. There are no calling hours.

Dunn Funeral Home 191 West St., Bristol, is in charge of the arrangements.
