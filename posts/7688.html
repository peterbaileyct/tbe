The American Rescue Plan Food Distribution Program will continue in September

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=7688 Published on 2021-09-03 10:35:04

The City of Bristol Mayor’s Office, United Way, the Connecticut Food Bank and Foodshare together are offering another round of ARPA Food Distribution in Bristol during the month of September. Thursdays, September 16 and 30. Distribution will take place at the Department of Public Works Garage on Vincent P Kelly Road from 4 pm-6 pm or until the boxes run out.
